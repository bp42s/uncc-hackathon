About NinerMarket

Our Story

NinerMarket is a sustainable, student-focused marketplace created specifically for college students at UNC Charlotte. Our platform enables students to buy, sell, and trade second-hand clothing, tech gadgets, and dorm essentials, fostering a community-driven space where students can find budget-friendly options and make environmentally conscious choices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a closed-loop economy on campus by encouraging students to choose pre-owned items. With NinerMarket, students can easily find the items they need while supporting sustainability and minimizing waste. We believe in empowering students to make responsible choices that positively impact both their wallets and the environment.

Why NinerMarket?

Designed by students, for students, NinerMarket addresses the unique needs of the college community, especially during move-in and move-out seasons. Our Team

We are a team of students participating in Hack CLT, blending technology and sustainability to bring NinerMarket to life. Our passion for eco-friendly practices drives us to create a platform that reduces waste and promotes responsible consumption within the UNC Charlotte community.

Thank you for being part of NinerMarket. Together, let's make sustainable choices and build a better future, one campus at a time!
