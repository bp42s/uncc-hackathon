Niner Market

Welcome to Niner Market! Our platform is designed specifically for college students, offering a sustainable, student-focused marketplace. Here, you can buy and sell everything from clothing and tech gadgets to dorm essentials, making sustainable choices convenient, affordable, and impactful for our campus community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a sustainable, circular economy on campus by encouraging students to choose pre-owned items. With Niner Market, you can find items you need and sell items you no longer use, creating a community-driven space where sustainability meets affordability.

Why It’s Useful

Niner Market is built for students’ unique needs, especially during transitions like moving in or out of dorms. It’s the perfect place for third-year students moving out to sell furniture, clothing, or electronics, and for new students to find essentials at budget-friendly prices.

Our Team

We’re a group of students passionate about sustainability and technology, participating in Hack CLT to bring Niner Market to life. With skills in development, design, and environmental science, we’re excited to provide a platform that keeps items circulating within our campus community, promoting responsible consumption.

Thank you for being a part of Niner Market. Together, we’re making our campus more sustainable, one item at a time!
